How to read AQI
0–50
Good
Air quality is satisfactory. Go outside freely.
51–100
Satisfactory
Acceptable. Sensitive people should be cautious.
101–200
Moderate
May cause breathing discomfort with prolonged exposure.
201–300
Poor
Breathing discomfort for most. Avoid outdoor activity.
301–400
Very Poor
Respiratory illness on prolonged exposure. Stay indoors.
401–500
Severe
Health alert. Everyone should avoid all outdoor activity.
